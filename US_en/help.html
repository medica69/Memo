MEMO - mind game

Object of the game:

You must guess four colours chosen by computer.

How to play:

You input your choice by putting pieces from game box (or later from table) in to the first empty row in table.

Selected colour is presented with shaded pieces.

When you finish selecting the combination of four colours you must press the next row button to get the result.

Computer gives the result with black and white pieces

- one black piece means that one piece have right colour and is in the right place.

- one white piece means that one piece have right colour but isn't in the right place.

How to quit:

If you want to play another game just click reset button.

If you don't want to play anymore just go to different location or close browser window.

P.S.: All browser windows created with this game will be automatically closed.
